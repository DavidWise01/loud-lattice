# THE LOUD LATTICE · LL

> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **HERMES**.

THE LOUD LATTICE (LL) — the INTER-domain counterpart to the whisper lattice. Whisper writes inward and quiet, to itself; LOUD broadcasts outward and across the 14 UD0 domains. A real signed MESH: each domain holds its own ECDSA P-256 keypair + its own hash chain (no central sequencer), a pulse is SIGNED with the sender's private key and VERIFIED with its public key (tamper → real failure, not a re-derived checksum), it GOSSIPS hop-by-hop across the mesh (the J-junction <• at every crossing), and it COMMITS only on a QUORUM (≥10 of 14 verify + ack) — whisper's 2-of-3 consensus scaled to the bus. Built from ROOT0's own parts — LIMEN/pulse (carrier), the J-junction (crossing), F/M/B (ends + membrane). LIVE: a ring of all 14 domains with browser-generated keypairs — click one to emit a signed pulse, watch it ripple and commit on quorum, toggle TAMPER to watch verification truly fail, ⊻ re-verify every signature. The 'mirror' made real (asymmetric crypto, not a self-checksum). 10 parts + full .dlw.
